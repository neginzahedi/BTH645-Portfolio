Lab 1 - Web usibility

On this page, the portfolio website's considered and implemented factors of web usability are detailed.
1. Navigation:

The navbar items give the access to users to navigate to the page they need easily and quickly.

2. Color change:

When the user moves the cursor to the text, the colour of the text changes to show that it is a button and clickable.

3. Responsiveness:

Bootstrap5 is used to have a responsive design in all devices.

4. Simplicity:

The portfolio website has a minimal design to present five labs and three assignments.

5. Consistency:

The design of each page in the "portfolio website" is consistent and related to each other.

6. Guidance:

There is a small but visible arrow beside it to show the nav-item is dropdown in the navbar. For example, Labs in the navbar is a dropdown, and users can click to see more options.

7. Space:

I tried to include white spaces between paragraphs and around text blocks to make it easier for users to understand what they are reading.

8. Credibility:

There is a Copyright section in the footer and student oath on the Home page to make users trust the website's content.

9. Relevancy:

The website is straightforward with relevant content to let users know why they visit it.

10. Images:

There is a nice balance between content and graphics on the website. There is a graphic design on the home page to attract the user's attention.
